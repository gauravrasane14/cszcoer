ASSOCIATION OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING STUDENTS (ACES)


Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES)
Departement of Computer Engineering

The Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) is a dynamic student body dedicated to enhancing the academic and extracurricular experience of computer engineering students at Zeal College of Engineering and Research. ACES serves as a platform for students to come together, collaborate, and engage in activities that foster both professional and personal development. The association organizes workshops, technical seminars, coding competitions, and guest lectures from industry experts, providing students with opportunities to expand their knowledge and stay updated with the latest technological trends. Through events like hackathons and project exhibitions, ACES encourages innovation, teamwork, and practical problem-solving skills. Additionally, ACES plays a pivotal role in bridging the gap between students and the industry, offering valuable networking opportunities and helping students connect with potential career pathways. Overall, ACES is an essential part of the computer department, creating a thriving community where students can excel academically and professionally.
"The power of a student association lies in its ability to unite diverse voices, amplify collective strength, and drive change that shapes both the present and future of the community."

Events Conducted by ACES

Engineers Day 2024

Date: September 10, 2024

The Engineers' Day event, organized by ACES at Zeal College of Engineering and Research, is a celebration of the contributions of engineers to society and the advancement of technology. This event features a variety of activities such as guest lectures, technical workshops, competitions, and presentations that highlight the importance of engineering in shaping the future. The event aims to inspire students by showcasing the achievements of renowned engineers and providing a platform for them to engage with industry professionals.

ACES Team Event

Date: March 10, 2024

The ACES Team Event 2024 was an exciting celebration of collaboration and creativity. With engaging activities, team-building challenges, and fun competitions, members showcased their skills and strengthened bonds. It was a day filled with energy, enthusiasm, and teamwork, reinforcing the spirit of unity and collaboration within the ACES community.

Women's Day Self Defence Workshop

Date: March 08, 2024

ACES celebrated Women's Day by hosting a self-defense workshop aimed at empowering women with essential skills for personal safety. The workshop included practical techniques, confidence-building exercises, and awareness strategies, fostering a sense of security and self-assurance among participants. It was a meaningful way to honor the occasion.

Fresher's Party 2024

Date: January 10, 2024

ACES hosted an unforgettable Freshers' Party 2024, welcoming new students with excitement and energy. The event featured music, dancing, and interactive games, allowing freshers to bond, make new friends, and immerse themselves in the vibrant campus culture. It was a perfect start to their academic journey.
